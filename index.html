Indian Samosa Day

Today, we celebrate the delicious and versatile samosa. Samosas are a popular Indian snack or appetizer made with a savory filling wrapped in a fried pastry shell. The filling can vary depending on the region, but it typically includes potatoes, peas, and spices. Samosas are often served with chutney or raita.
