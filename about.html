ABOUT US

SpicyHut is a family-owned and operated Indian restaurant that has been serving the community for over 20 years. We are passionate about providing our guests with an authentic and delicious Indian dining experience. Our menu features a wide variety of traditional Indian dishes, all made with fresh, high-quality ingredients and prepared with care by our experienced chefs.
We are proud to offer a warm and inviting atmosphere where guests can relax and enjoy a meal with friends and family. Whether you are a regular customer or visiting us for the first time, we hope you will feel at home at SpicyHut.
